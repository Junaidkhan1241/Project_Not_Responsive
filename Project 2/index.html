What is an online group study?

A virtual study group is a group of students who come together
online to study and learn collaboratively.
It allows students to work together on academic projects,
assignments, or test preparation without the need to be
in the same physical location.

How can I make the most out of online group study sessions?

1.Try to keep it small. To keep organizing things simple, try not to let your study group get too big.
2.Choose a time to meet.
3.Keep the meetings short.
4.Choose a place to meet.
5.Have a session moderator.
6.Decide on a purpose/set a plan.
7.Set rules.
8.Stay focused.
